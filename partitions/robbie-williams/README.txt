Robbie Williams Chord and Key Annotation Dataset.

------

Home page of the dataset: http://home.deib.polimi.it/zanoni/Site/Research.html 
(check for updates)

If you decide to use the dataset on your study we kindly ask to cite the following paper:

Bruno di Giorgi, Massimiliano Zanoni, Augusto Sarti, Stefano Tubaro 
“Automatic chord recognition based on the probabilistic modeling of diatonic modal harmony”, 
in Proceedings of the 8th international workshop on multidimensional (nD) systems - nDS13 - 2013, Erlangen, Germany
